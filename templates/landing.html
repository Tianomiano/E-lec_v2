About E-lec

Inspiration

I come from a biochemistry background.During my undergraduate questions for assignments would be from different academic fields and the available online resources were minimal hence a need to interact with students from those fields. This is quite challenging without their contacts. E-lec is designed to bridge this gap allowing students from different fields to share their opinions about topics and be part of the discussions.
